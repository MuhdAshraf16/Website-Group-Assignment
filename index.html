About Us

History fo NipponKing Sushi

The restaurant was established in 2018 by our main chef and his friends with initially only on a small scale. After some time, our restaurant is growing and becoming an option for customers who want to eat sushi with good quality.

Our sushi restaurant provides food from ingredients that are guaranteed clean and high quality at reasonable prices to suit all of customers. In addition, our restaurant is special because it provides a service to choose the menu of choice and directly made by our chef to be eaten fresh from the kitchen.

Our chefs have a wide range of experience in sushi making and have studied with a well-known sushi maker in Japan. In addition, they also have a sushi making approval certificate as well as a halal certificate that customers do not have to worry or doubt about their skills and hygiene level.
